Although we have cancelled the weekly Prewired sessions until Covid-19 lockdown measures are lifted, we are now launching Prewired Online! The next session takes place 17:30–18:30 on Wednesday 15 April.

If you are on our mailing list, you will have received an initial email announcement with the subject line "Prewired Online" and instructions for registering on Eventbrite. A link for joining the session will be sent out to all registered participants.

Questions? Contact us at hello@prewired.org.
